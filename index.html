About Plus N Chic

Bold, colorful, design-led

Plusnchic is your online plus size boutique for all plus and chic dresses for the curvy lady. Our store is a must visit and we cater for sizes 14-28 with collections that are bold and colourful, packed with design-led pieces. As a size 18/20 I have struggled finding clothing that actually fitted me and managing this online store will enable me source clothing that will actually excite and solve other plus size closet issues. Take all the hassle out of deciding what to wear whatever your budget covering everything DRESS. Our objective remains to make you feel confident, gorgeous and fearless
